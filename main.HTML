Our Research Hints

We are a group of students studying how traditional learning and online learning compare.

We look at how a mixed style, called adaptive learning, can combine both to help students learn better.

We are also interested in games. We compare traditional games like volleyball and basketball with online games like first-person shooters, building games, and other genres.

Our livestream will give small clues about what we learn instead of showing all of the details at once.
